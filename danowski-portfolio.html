About

The rare designer who also builds — and makes.

I'm a South Jersey-based designer, developer, and maker with over 15 years building visual identities, digital products, and hand-crafted objects that last. My background spans graphic design, front-end development, and physical fabrication — which means I can take a brand from concept to code to the real world.

Past clients include Villanova University Athletics and the Philadelphia 76ers. Current work includes reverse gilded glass signs, custom software systems, and a co-founded product company pursuing a patented magnetic glasses hinge.
