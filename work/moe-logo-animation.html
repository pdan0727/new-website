Moe Logo Animation

CategoryMotion Graphics / Branding
DeliverableLogo Animation
ToolsAdobe After Effects, Illustrator

Animated logo sting for Moe. The brief called for a motion piece that brought the existing mark to life — giving it energy and personality suitable for use as an intro/outro on video content and social media.

The animation was designed to be punchy and short, with timing and easing that reinforces the character of the brand rather than just moving elements for movement's sake.
