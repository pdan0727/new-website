Magni-Frames — Magnetic Glasses Hinge

CompanyAnomalous
CategoryProduct / Branding
StatusPatent Pending
DeliverablesPatent Filing, Brand Identity, Web Design

Magni-Frames is a patented magnetic hinge system for eyeglass frames, developed under Anomalous — a small product company founded with partners Solomon and Dominic. The core innovation replaces traditional spring hinges with precision magnets, allowing the temples to snap securely into place and release safely on impact.

Work on this project spans the patent documentation and illustrations, brand identity for both Magni-Frames and Anomalous, and web design for the product launch. The company is currently exploring licensing opportunities and direct-to-market options.
