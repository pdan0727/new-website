Logo Design / Branding

Thumper Products

ClientThumper Products, LLC
CategoryLogo Design / Identity
LocationSouthampton, NJ
ToolsAdobe Illustrator

Brand identity for Thumper Products LLC, makers of the Close Combat Hand Guard — a tactical accessory for military and law enforcement applications. The logo needed to project authority and durability while remaining clean enough to work across print, web, and product applications.

The mark combines military-influenced typography with a patriotic emblem, giving the brand immediate visual credibility in the tactical market. Delivered alongside a full business card design.
